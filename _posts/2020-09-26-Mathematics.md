## Basic Fomula

Given a mapping: $f: R^N \rightarrow R^M$ and input X with dimension N, output with dimension M,
we we call the matrix with following form a Jocobian Matrix:
$$
\frac{\partial{y}}{\partial{x}} = \begin{bmatrix}
    0 & 0 & 1 \\
    1 & 1 & 0 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
